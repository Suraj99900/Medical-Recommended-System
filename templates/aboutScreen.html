About

Most drivers understand the dangers of drinking and driving and texting and driving, but many people underestimate the dangers of drowsy driving. Each year, drowsy driving accounts for about 100,000 crashes, 71,000 injuries and 1,550 fatalities, according to the National Safety Council (NSC).

VISION

To build software that will be useful for the society by building a team of hardworking and passionate software developer who are ready to work as a team and not afraid of taking responsibilities.

The main reason, we started to develop this application is the eye grabbing increment of the number of road accident. using the concept of machine learning we developed this application safety of every driver and his/her passengers is our main goal.
